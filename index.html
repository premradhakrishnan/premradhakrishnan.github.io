Keeping it succinct

Everyone is time poor and my aim for this blog is to keep things as succinct as possible.
Therefore no apologies for the generous use of bullets you happen to see.

Life would be so much simpler if I had the source code.
~ Anonymous

With all my posts and code snippets, I will only put up things I have tried and tested and I will endeavour to document the steps leading up to them.

I hope I get to use the content in these myself and that it also brings value to anyone else stopping by.

Cheers.
